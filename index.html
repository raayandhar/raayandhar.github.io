Raayan Dhar

I am an undergraduate studying Computer Science with a concentration in Electrical Engineering at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Currently, I do research on robotics. This fall, I will be working on the IERE compiler at AMD as a compiler engineer intern. Before that, I will be spending the summer working at NVIDIA as a deep learning inference intern on TensorRT-LLM.

Over summer 2024 I was a software development intern at Amazon on their low-latency risk evaluation service. Far before that, my interest in computer science stemmed from a summer working on Monte-Carlo simulations at the Paegal Lab.

Interests

I am most interested in deep learning systems for the real world, across the entire stack (theory to architecture to low-level optimization). These days, I work on robot learning, namely imitation learning.
